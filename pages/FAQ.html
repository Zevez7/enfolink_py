Are there any HIPAA concerns?

  • Absolutely not; no protected Patient Health information is ever collected through Enfolink.

  • Patients access media anonymously through enfolink.org using a randomly generated access code.

  • Giving a patient a bundle is very similar to giving a patient a pamphlet or list of website URLs, but much more convenient for both you and your patients.

Is it really free?

  • Yes. 100% free. There are no ads and no subscription fees. We do not sell your data to any third parties.

  • In the future, we might charge for certain premium features, but all currently-available features will remain free forever.

Do I have to create a bundle for every patient?

  • No, you can just simply create a “default” bundle that you hand to multiple patients with similar health concerns.

  • However, giving a unique bundle to every patient allows you to check whether or not they have accessed it.

Will my patient see ads on Enfolink?

  • No patients will ever see ads when accessing their bundles through Enfolink.org. It’s a promise.

Why do you need to register with a name, work email address, and place of work?

  • We want to make sure that you’re a licensed health professional.

  • This will ensure that content shared on the site is rated and reviewed by a community of qualified individuals.

How long do the created bundles last?

  • They will be available to your patients for as long as you have an active account.

  • Deleting a bundle will instantly disable access to it.

Can I add my own content to Enfolink?

  • Yes, in fact we encourage it!

  • Content added to Enfolink will be accessible to everyone that uses Enfolink.

Do you provide help to get started?

  • Check the “Learn” page to see some video instructions for using the site.

  • If you need more assistance, please send an email to Dat@enfolink.org
